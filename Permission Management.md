# Permission Management
**Permission management** controls who can **read, write, or execute** files and directories.

1. r ----------> read (4)  
2. w -----------> write (2)      
3. x -----------> execute (1) 
4. (-) -----------> no permission (0)
 - total sum is 7

. chmod ---------- to change permission
. chown ----------- to change owner
. chgrp ------------ to change group
